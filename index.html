Place points on the requested item(s).

Use the subject's left and right, not yours.

Use the point tool to place points on the requested target(s) of interest:

  1. Place a point in the center of the item using your mouse over each instance of the target.
  2. Do not include parts of the target that cannot be seen, even though you think you can interpolate the whole shape of the target.
  3. Use the subject's left and right, not yours.
  4. Avoid shadows, they're not considered as a part of the target.
