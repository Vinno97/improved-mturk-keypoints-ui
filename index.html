Use the point tool to place points on the requested joints:

  • Place a point in the center of the item using your mouse over each instance of the target.
  • Do not include body parts that are occluded by another limb or object.
  • Use the subject's left and right, not yours.
  • In case multiple persons are visible, the subject is the child whose profile is best indicated by the red outline. In most cases this child is located centrally in the box.
  • The red boxes are automatically generated but manually verified. In some cases we have manually indicated which child is the subject for clarity purposes.
  • Make sure all labelled points belong to the same child.
  • It is very important that you have the correct label selected when placing a point.
